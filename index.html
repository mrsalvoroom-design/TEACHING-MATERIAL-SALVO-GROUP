1

PART OF SPEECH IN GENERAL

8 Jenis Kata Dasar dalam Bahasa Inggris yang Wajib Dikuasai

Tujuan Pembelajaran: Memastikan peserta hafal nama Part Of Speech dan terangkan secara sekilas kegunaannya.

1. VERB (Kata Kerja)

Kata yang menunjukkan tindakan, keadaan, atau proses. Contoh: run, eat, sleep, is, become.

Contoh:

She runs every morning.
They are happy.

2. NOUN (Kata Benda)

Kata yang menamai orang, tempat, benda, atau konsep. Contoh: book, Jakarta, teacher, love.

Contoh:

The book is on the table.
Love is beautiful.

3. PRONOUN (Kata Ganti)

Kata yang menggantikan noun untuk menghindari pengulangan. Contoh: I, you, he, she, it, they.

Contoh:

She is a doctor.
I gave it to them.

4. ADJECTIVE (Kata Sifat)

Kata yang menerangkan atau memberi keterangan tentang noun. Contoh: beautiful, tall, expensive.

Contoh:

A beautiful girl.
The tall building.

5. ADVERB (Kata Keterangan)

Kata yang menerangkan verb, adjective, atau adverb lain. Contoh: quickly, very, always, here.

Contoh:

She runs quickly.
He is very tall.

6. PREPOSITION (Kata Depan)

Kata yang menunjukkan hubungan antara kata dengan kata lain. Contoh: in, on, at, under, between.

Contoh:

The book is on the table.
She lives in Jakarta.

7. CONJUNCTION (Kata Hubung)

Kata yang menghubungkan kata, frasa, atau klausa. Contoh: and, but, or, because, although.

Contoh:

I like tea and coffee.
He is tired but happy.

8. INTERJECTION (Kata Seru)

Kata yang mengungkapkan emosi atau perasaan tiba-tiba. Contoh: Wow!, Ouch!, Hurray!, Oh!

Contoh:

Wow! That's amazing!
Ouch! That hurts!

🔑 KATA KUNCI: DO, HAVE, dan Kata Ganti Orang ke-3 Tunggal

Perubahan DO → DOES

  • I do → He/She/It does
  • You do → They do
  • We do → Does your friend...?

Perubahan HAVE → HAS

  • I have → He/She/It has
  • You have → They have
  • We have → She has bought...

📋 POSSESSIVE PRONOUN (Kata Ganti Kepemilikan)

Menunjukkan kepemilikan, bukan Subjek atau Objek!

My / Mine (Milikku)
This is my book. / This book is mine.
Your / Yours (Milikmu)
Is this your pen? / This pen is yours.
Our / Ours (Milik kita)
This is our house. / This house is ours.
Their / Theirs (Milik mereka)
That's their car. / That car is theirs.
His (Milik dia lk)
This is his laptop. / This laptop is his.
Her / Hers (Milik dia pr)
That is her bag. / That bag is hers.
Contoh Penting: The cat is wagging its tail. (Bukan "it's" = it is)

SUBJECT & PREDICATE

Struktur Dasar Kalimat Bahasa Inggris

Konsep Dasar: Peserta harus memahami konsep dasar struktur kalimat Bahasa Inggris. Setiap kalimat minimal memiliki Subject (S) dan Predicate (P).

VERBAL

Ada yang dilakukan (ada kata kerja/Verb)

Formula:
S + V + O

Subject + Verb + Object

Jenis Verbal:

Transitif (S + V + O)

Melakukan apa terhadap benda/orang

  • I move the glass
  • I kick him
  • She eats an apple
Intransitif (S + V + C)

Menerangkan tanpa objek langsung

  • I feel sad (Adjective)
  • I feel happy (Adjective)
  • I slept all day (Adverb)

NOMINAL

Tidak ada yang dilakukan (tidak ada kata kerja/Verb)

Formula:
S + To Be + ANA

Subject + To Be + Adjective/Noun/Adverb

Bentuk To Be:

Is
He/She/It
Am
I
Are
You/We/They
Was
I/He/She/It
Were
You/We/They
Be/Been
Bentuk lain

Contoh Nominal:

  • I am a student (Noun)
  • She is beautiful (Adjective)
  • They are here (Adverb)
  • He was tired yesterday (Adjective)

PHRASE AND CLAUSE

Memahami Struktur Kalimat yang Lebih Kompleks

Pentingnya: Peserta harus memahami konsep dasar struktur kalimat Bahasa Inggris, memahami apa itu Clausa. 1 Klausa = 1 Tenses

✅ INDEPENDENT CLAUSE

Berdiri sendiri, sederajat (Main Clause)

Ciri-ciri:
  • Memiliki subject dan predicate lengkap
  • Dapat berdiri sendiri sebagai kalimat
  • Dapat dihubungkan dengan conjunction

Contoh:

  • I like writing S+P
  • I like reading S+P
  • I don't like studying S+P
Penggabungan:
"I like writing, and reading, but I don't like studying"
Tetap menjadi 3 klausa walaupun menggunakan Conjunction

🔗 DEPENDENT CLAUSE

Bergantung, tidak sederajat (Subordinate Clause)

Ciri-ciri:
  • Memiliki subject dan predicate
  • Tidak dapat berdiri sendiri
  • Membutuhkan independent clause
  • Diawali conjunction (when, if, because, dll)

Contoh:

  • When Jerry gets here, let's start the music.
  • The man who was standing next to me smelled really bad.
  • I will go if you come with me.
Identifikasi: Cari kata penghubung seperti when, who, that, because, if, until, where.

🎯 TEKNIK SPEAKING BERDASARKAN KLAUSA

1. TEKNIK "ARE"

Answer, Reason, Example

Jawab → Beri alasan → Beri contoh

2. TEKNIK PPF

Past, Present, Future

Cerita masa lalu → Sekarang → Mendatang

3. TEKNIK 5 KLAUSA

Narasi Lengkap

Opening → Bridging → Main Idea → Reason → Closing

📝 LATIHAN: Identifikasi Klausa & Tenses

"I checked my room and I found a diamond ring under the pillow. I believe it belongs to my wife, so I will keep this ring for her until she gets back. Maybe she forgot where she put her ring, I will keep this ring because I don't want to buy her a new one."

Analisis: Ada berapa klausa di atas? Jika sudah berhasil memecah klausa, maka sekarang peserta akan dapat melihat ada berapa tenses di dalamnya (1 klausa = 1 tenses).

VERBAL & NOMINAL

Bentuk Kalimat dengan dan tanpa Kata Kerja

Perbedaan Utama: Verbal menggunakan kata kerja (Verb), sedangkan Nominal menggunakan To Be tanpa kata kerja aktif.

VERBAL

Ciri Khas:
  • Menggunakan kata kerja aktif (V1, V2, V3, V-ing)
  • Menunjukkan tindakan/aksi
  • Ada objek (transitif) atau keterangan (intransitif)

Struktur:

S + V + O/C

Contoh Verbal:

  • They work in the office
  • She eats an apple every day
  • I move the glass
  • He kicked the ball yesterday
  • We have finished the job
Tip: Jika ada kata kerja aktif (run, eat, make, do, dll), itu adalah kalimat Verbal!

NOMINAL

Ciri Khas:
  • Menggunakan To Be (is, am, are, was, were)
  • Tidak ada kata kerja aktif
  • Menerangkan keadaan/sifat/lokasi
  • Diikuti Adjektiva, Nomina, atau Adverb

Struktur:

S + To Be + ANA

(Adjective, Noun, Adverb)

Contoh Nominal:

  • I am happy (Adjective)
  • She is a teacher (Noun)
  • They are here (Adverb)
  • He was sick yesterday (Adjective)
  • We were students (Noun)
Tip: Jika ada To Be + kata sifat/tempat/benda (bukan kata kerja), itu adalah kalimat Nominal!

⚡ PERBANDINGAN LANGSUNG

VERBAL (Aktif) NOMINAL (Keadaan)
She eats an apple. She is hungry.
They play football. They are happy.
I wrote a letter. I was busy.
He will go to school. He will be ready.

V1 - V2 - V3

Bentuk dan Penggunaan Verb 1, Verb 2, dan Verb 3

Tujuan: Peserta harus memahami penggunaan V1, V2, V3 dalam berbagai konteks kalimat.

VERB 1

Bare Infinitive / Kata Kerja Dasar

Penggunaan:
  • Routine (Rutinitas)
  • Habits (Kebiasaan)
  • True Fact (Fakta)
  • Schedule (Jadwal)
S + V1 + O
Aturan Khusus:

V1 + s/es/ies jika Subject He/She/It

  • He works every day
  • She watches TV
  • It flies high
Modal + V1:
Do/Does/Did + V1
Modal Verb (will, can, may, must) + V1

VERB 2

Simple Past / Past Tense

Penggunaan:
  • Kejadian masa lampau
  • Sudah selesai di masa lampau
  • Keterangan waktu lampau
S + V2 + O
Jenis V2:

Regular: Berakhiran -ed/d

  • work → worked
  • play → played

Irregular: Tidak beraturan

  • go → went
  • eat → ate
  • buy → bought
Contoh:
I fixed my car last night.
I watched TV 3 hours ago.

VERB 3

Past Participle / Kata Kerja Ketiga

Penggunaan:
  • Perfect: Have/Has/Had + V3
    (Telah/sudah/belum)
  • Passive: To Be + V3
    (Awalan di-/ter-)
Have/Has/Had + V3
To Be + V3
"Bunglon":

Bisa menjadi bentuk:

  • Present Perfect
  • Past Perfect
  • Future Perfect
  • Passive Voice
Contoh:
I have fixed my car.
I had watched TV.
The car is fixed by mechanic.

Bentuk Rumus Contoh Arti

Simple Past S + V2 She worked Dia bekerja (kemarin)
Present Perfect Has/Have + V3 She has worked Dia telah bekerja

ACTIVE & PASSIVE

Menguasai Bentuk Aktif dan Pasif dalam Kalimat

Pemahaman: Menguasai bentuk Active dan Passive voice serta perubahan keterangan waktunya.

✅ ACTIVE VOICE

Subjek melakukan tindakan (Pelaku)

Ciri:
  • Subjek = Pelaku
  • Objek = Penerima tindakan
  • Lebih langsung dan umum digunakan
S + V + O

Contoh:

  • The mechanic fixes the car.
  • My mother cooked dinner.
  • They will build a house.

🔄 PASSIVE VOICE

Subjek menerima tindakan (Bukan pelaku)

Ciri:
  • Subjek = Penerima tindakan
  • By + Object = Pelaku (boleh dihilangkan)
  • Menggunakan To Be + V3
S + To Be + V3 + (by O)

Contoh:

  • The car is fixed (by the mechanic).
  • Dinner was cooked (by my mother).
  • A house will be built (by them).

📊 TABEL PERUBAHAN ACTIVE → PASSIVE

Tenses Active Passive Arti
Simple Present S + V1 Is/Am/Are + V3 Dikerjakan
The car is fixed by a mechanic.
They are not allowed to enter.
Simple Past S + V2 Was/Were + V3 Dikerjakan (lampau)
The car was fixed by a mechanic yesterday.
They were not allowed last night.
Simple Future S + Will + V1 Will + Be + V3 Akan dikerjakan
The car will be fixed tomorrow.
They will be allowed next week.

⚠️ KATA TANDA PASSIVE (Bahasa Indonesia)

Awalan di-

dikerjakan, dimakan, dibuat

Awalan ter-

terbuka, tertutup, terlihat

Awalan -di (lokasi)

di Jakarta, di sekolah

PART OF SPEECH IN DETAILS

Pembahasan Mendetail Setiap Jenis Kata

Tujuan: Memahami bentuk Part Of Speech secara mendetail, dibahas satu per satu dengan fungsi dan contoh yang lengkap.

1. VERB (Kata Kerja) - Detail Lengkap

Jenis Verb:

  • Action Verb: run, eat, write
  • Linking Verb: be, become, seem
  • Helping Verb: do, have, will

Bentuk Verb:

  • V1: eat, go, write
  • V2: ate, went, wrote
  • V3: eaten, gone, written
  • V-ing: eating, going

2. NOUN (Kata Benda) - Detail Lengkap

Proper Noun

Nama khusus

Indonesia, John, Monday

Common Noun

Nama umum

country, person, day

Abstract Noun

Konsep abstrak

love, happiness, idea

Countable vs Uncountable: book/books (bisa dihitung) vs water, rice (tidak bisa dihitung langsung)

3. PRONOUN (Kata Ganti) - Detail Lengkap

Tipe Contoh Fungsi
Subject Pronoun I, You, He, She, It, We, They Sebagai pelaku
Object Pronoun Me, You, Him, Her, It, Us, Them Sebagai objek
Possessive Adjective My, Your, His, Her, Its, Our, Their Menjelaskan kepemilikan
Possessive Pronoun Mine, Yours, His, Hers, Ours, Theirs Menggantikan noun
Reflexive Pronoun Myself, Yourself, Himself, Herself Kembali ke subjek

4. ADJECTIVE (Kata Sifat) - Detail Lengkap

Urutan Adjective:

1. Opinion (beautiful)

2. Size (big)

3. Age (old)

4. Shape (round)

5. Color (red)

6. Origin (Indonesian)

7. Material (wooden)

8. Purpose (writing)

Jenis Adjective:

  • Descriptive: beautiful, tall
  • Quantitative: many, few, several
  • Demonstrative: this, that, these
  • Possessive: my, your, his
  • Interrogative: which, what, whose
Contoh Urutan:

"A beautiful big old round red Indonesian wooden writing desk"

5. ADVERB (Kata Keterangan) - Detail Lengkap

Adverb of Manner

Cara

quickly, slowly, well

Adverb of Time

Waktu

now, yesterday, soon

Adverb of Place

Tempat

here, there, everywhere

Adverb of Frequency

Frekuensi

always, often, never

Adverb of Degree

Tingkatan

very, quite, almost

Interrogative Adv

Pertanyaan

when, where, why

6. PREPOSITION (Kata Depan)

Menunjukkan hubungan tempat, waktu, atau arah.

Tempat: in, on, at, under, between, behind

Waktu: at 5 o'clock, on Monday, in 2024

Arah: to, from, into, through

7. CONJUNCTION (Kata Hubung)

Menghubungkan kata, frasa, atau klausa.

Coordinating: for, and, nor, but, or, yet, so (FANBOYS)

Subordinating: because, although, if, when

Correlative: either...or, neither...nor, both...and

8. INTERJECTION (Kata Seru)

Mengungkapkan emosi tiba-tiba. Biasanya diikuti tanda seru (!)

Hurray!

🔍 KATA KUNCI WAKTU (Time Signal)

Simple Present

  • Every day/week
  • Always, usually
  • Often, sometimes
  • Never, rarely

Simple Past

  • Yesterday
  • ... ago (2 days ago)
  • Last week/month
  • In 2020

Present Perfect

  • Already, recently
  • Yet (negasi/tanya)
  • Just, lately
  • Since, for
  • Ever, never

Continuous

  • Now, right now
  • At the moment
  • At 5 o'clock yesterday
  • When... (interrupted)

Simple Future

  • Tomorrow
  • Next week/year
  • Soon, later
  • In 2025

Past Perfect

  • Before, after
  • By the time
  • Already (past)
  • When... (completed)

🎯 CONDITIONAL SENTENCES (Bonus)

Type If Clause Main Clause Makna
Type 0 Simple Present (V1) Simple Present (V1) Fakta umum

📝 CAUSATIVE (Bonus Materi)

Menyuruh/meminta orang lain melakukan sesuatu.

Let, Make, Have (Tanpa To)

Rumus: S + Have/Make/Let + Person + V1

  • Mr. Salvo had me do the homework.
  • I made him clean the room.
  • She let me use her car.

Get, Ask (Dengan To)

Rumus: S + Get/Ask + Person + To + V1

  • I will get someone to repair my watch.
  • I asked him to help me.
  • She got me to finish the report.
